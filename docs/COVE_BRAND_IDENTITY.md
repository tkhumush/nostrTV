# Brand Identity: Cove
**Status:** Post-Discovery / Strategy Phase
**Core Concept:** The Digital Hearth / A Sheltered Sanctuary
**Target Platform:** tvOS & Google TV

---

## 1. The Vision Statement
> "In the vast, noisy ocean of the internet, Cove is your sheltered spot. It's the place where you kick off your shoes, grab a drink, and tune into the people who matter—live, unfiltered, and right in your living room."

## 2. Brand Personality & Voice
**The Persona:** The Friendly Neighbor
**The Vibe:** Warm, communal, approachable, and cozy.
**Voice Guidelines:**
* **Simple over Technical:** Avoid jargon (relays, keys, protocols). Focus on the experience.
* **Inviting over Demanding:** Use phrases that feel like an invitation to join a conversation rather than a command to "consume content."
* **Tactile over Abstract:** Use metaphors that feel physical and grounded (e.g., "pulling up a chair").

### Interface Copy Examples:
| System Event | Friendly Neighbor Voice |
| :--- | :--- |
| **App Loading** | "Preparing your spot by the fire..." |
| **No Streams Live** | "The tide is low right now. Check back soon!" |
| **Follow Creator** | "Pull up a chair" or "Stay in touch." |
| **Zap/Tip** | "Drop a pearl" or "Send some warmth." |

---

## 3. Visual Language
Designed specifically for the "10-foot experience" (viewing from a couch).

### Color Palette (The "Twilight" Scheme)
* **Deep Harbor (#1A1F2C):** A soft, desaturated navy for the background. Easier on the eyes than pure black for long-term TV viewing.
* **Sea Foam (#A3D9C9):** A muted, friendly mint used for primary buttons and selection states.
* **Sunset Gold (#FFB347):** A warm, glowing amber used for "Live" indicators and value-exchange (Zaps).

### Typography
* **Primary (Headings):** *Outfit* or *Lexend* (Rounded, friendly sans-serif).
* **Secondary (Body):** *Inter* (Highly legible at a distance).

### Iconography
* **Rounded Geometry:** No sharp corners. Everything should feel "worn smooth" like a sea stone.
* **The Live Indicator:** A pulsing amber glow (the "spark") rather than a harsh red recording light.

---

## 4. The Grand Vision
Cove isn't just an app; it's a **Lifestyle Bridge**. It takes the decentralized power of **nostr** and wraps it in a "Digital Furniture" experience. It aims to replace the "lonely scroll" of mobile apps with a "shared glow" in the home's most central gathering place: the living room.

---

## 5. TV-Specific Experience Goals
* **The 3-Click Rule:** Users should be able to get from the home screen to a live stream in 3 clicks or fewer.
* **Ambient Mode:** When idle, the app should function as a "Digital Fireplace," showing muted, beautiful highlights of active streams.
* **Synchronicity:** Emphasis on *Real-Time*. If it isn't happening now, it isn't in the Cove.
